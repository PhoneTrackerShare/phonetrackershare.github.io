Ora Tracker Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: April 4th, 2026

Thank you for purchasing Ora Tracker. This policy explains how we handle refunds and cancellations. The process and eligibility for a refund depend on where you made your purchase: via the Apple App Store or directly from the Ora Tracker official website.

Policy for Purchases Made via Apple App Store

1. Provider of Sale

For all subscriptions purchased through the Ora Tracker app on an Apple device, Apple Inc. is the merchant of record. This means all billing, payments, and refunds are processed and controlled exclusively by Apple under their terms and conditions. We do not have the ability to process charges or issue refunds for these purchases.

2. How to Request a Refund from Apple

You must submit all refund requests directly to Apple. To do so, please visit Apple's problem reports page: https://reportaproblem.apple.com.

Sign in with your Apple ID, locate the Ora Tracker purchase, and follow the on-screen instructions to submit your request. The decision to grant a refund is at the sole discretion of Apple.

3. How to Cancel Your App Store Subscription

To prevent future charges, you must cancel your subscription's auto-renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

  • Open the Settings app > tap your name > tap Subscriptions > select Ora Tracker > tap Cancel Subscription.

Policy for Purchases Made via Our Website

1. Provider of Sale

For subscriptions purchased directly from the Ora Tracker website, we are the merchant of record. Our designated payment processor (e.g., Stripe, Paddle) handles the transactions, and we manage billing inquiries and refund requests according to the policy below.

2. Our 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for initial purchases made on our website. If you are not satisfied with our service for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your original purchase date.

3. How to Request a Refund from Us

To request a refund under our 7-day guarantee, please email our support team at phonetracker.feedback@outlook.com with the following information:

  • The email address used to make the purchase.
  • Your order number or transaction ID.
  • A brief reason for your refund request.

We will review your request and process eligible refunds within 5-7 business days.

4. Refund Eligibility for Website Purchases

A. Situations That Qualify for a Refund (within 7 days):

  • Unused Service within 7 Days: You request a refund within 7 days of the original purchase, AND our system records confirm that you have not used any core features of the service. "Use" is defined as logging in and performing any key action, such as adding a device, viewing location data, or setting up a geo-fence.
  • Critical Technical Failure: If the service is non-functional due to a verifiable technical fault from our end that we cannot resolve in a timely manner.
  • Accidental Duplicate Purchase: If you were accidentally charged twice for the same initial subscription on our website.

B. Situations Typically Not Eligible for a Refund:

  • After 7 Days: Any refund request submitted more than 7 days after the original purchase date.
  • Subscription Renewals: Our 7-day guarantee applies only to the initial purchase, not to automatic renewals. You are responsible for canceling your subscription before the renewal date if you do not wish to continue the service.
  • Forgetting to Cancel: We do not offer refunds for renewal charges resulting from a failure to cancel the subscription in time.
  • User-Caused Issues: Problems arising from denying necessary permissions, using an unsupported device, or user error.
  • Terms of Service Violation: No refunds will be issued if your account was terminated for violating our Terms of Service.

5. How to Cancel Your Website Subscription

To cancel a subscription purchased on our website and avoid future renewals, log in to your account dashboard on our official website. Navigate to the "Billing" or "Subscription" section and follow the instructions to cancel.
