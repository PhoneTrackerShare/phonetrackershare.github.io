Phone Tracker Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 21th, 2025

This policy applies exclusively to subscriptions purchased directly from the Phone Tracker official website. For purchases made through other platforms like the Apple App Store, please refer to their respective refund policies.

1. 14-Day Refund Policy

We offer a refund on your initial subscription purchase within 14 days of the transaction date, provided that the service has not been used.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email our support team at phonetracker.feedback@outlook.com with the following details:

  • Your reason for the refund request.
  • The email address you used to register and purchase.
  • The order number or transaction ID from your confirmation email.

Our team will review your request and verify that the service has not been activated or used. Eligible refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

3. Refund Eligibility Conditions

A. Situations That Qualify for a Refund:

  • Unused Service within 14 Days: You request a refund within 14 days of the original purchase, AND our system records confirm that you have not used any core features of the service. "Use" is defined as logging in and performing any key action, such as adding a device, viewing location data, or setting up a geo-fence.
  • Critical Technical Failure: The service is fundamentally non-functional due to a verifiable fault on our end that we cannot resolve within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Accidental Duplicate Purchase: You were accidentally charged more than once for the same subscription period.

B. Situations Typically Not Eligible for a Refund:

  • Any Use of the Service: If our records show that any core feature of the service was used, even once, the 14-day refund policy is voided.
  • Requests After 14 Days: Any refund request submitted more than 14 days after the original purchase date.
  • Subscription Renewals: Our refund policy applies only to the initial purchase of a subscription. It does not apply to any automatic renewals.
  • Forgetting to Cancel: We are not responsible for renewal charges if you fail to cancel your auto-renewing subscription before the renewal date.
  • User-Caused Issues: Problems arising from user error, an unsupported device, poor internet/GPS connection, or failure to grant necessary permissions on the target device.
  • Terms of Service Violation: No refunds will be issued if your account was suspended or terminated for violating our Terms of Service.

4. How to Cancel Your Subscription

You are responsible for canceling your subscription to prevent future charges. To do so, please log in to your account on our website. Navigate to the "Account" or "Billing" section and follow the instructions to cancel the subscription. This will stop all future automatic renewals.
