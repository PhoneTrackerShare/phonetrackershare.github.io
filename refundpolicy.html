Phone Tracker Refund, Cancellation, and Right of Withdrawal Policy

Last Updated: October 21th, 2025

1. Scope and Provider

This policy applies to all auto-renewing subscriptions for Premium Services within the Phone Tracker mobile application ("App"). The contractual partner for all purchases made via the Apple App Store is Apple Inc. ("Apple"). Phone Tracker is the provider of the App and its services but does not act as the merchant of record for transactions. All billing and refund matters are administered exclusively by Apple.

2. Conclusion of Contract and Consent

The subscription contract is concluded when you tap "Buy," "Subscribe," or an equivalent button within the Apple App Store in-app purchase interface and, if required, authenticate the purchase. By completing the purchase, you consent to the immediate commencement of the Premium Services.

3. Free Trials

If we offer a free trial, your subscription will automatically convert into a paid, auto-renewing subscription at the end of the trial period. To avoid being charged, you must cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the free trial ends. Free trials are typically limited to one per Apple ID.

4. Subscription Term, Auto-Renewal, and Cancellation

Your subscription will automatically renew for the same duration (e.g., monthly, yearly) unless you cancel it.

How to Cancel Auto-Renewal: You are solely responsible for canceling your subscription. You must do so at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period to prevent the next charge. To cancel, follow these steps:

  • Open your device's Settings app.
  • Tap on your name at the top.
  • Tap on Subscriptions.
  • Select the Phone Tracker subscription.
  • Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm.

Upon cancellation, your access to Premium Services will continue until the end of the current paid term.

5. Prices and Payment Conditions

All prices are displayed within the App Store interface before purchase. Apple conducts all payment processing, including the collection of applicable taxes. Phone Tracker does not handle payment or card information. Price changes for existing subscriptions will be communicated to you by Apple, and you may be required to agree to the new price to continue your subscription.

6. Refund Process and Policy

All refund requests must be made directly to Apple. As the merchant, Apple has its own policies and determines in its sole discretion whether a user is eligible for a refund. We are not technically or legally able to issue refunds for App Store purchases.

How to Officially Request a Refund:

  • Visit Apple's problem reports page: https://reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with the Apple ID used to make the purchase.
  • Find the Phone Tracker subscription charge and click the "Report" or "Report a Problem" button next to it.
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to describe the issue and submit your request.

Apple typically reviews requests within a few days. The decision to grant a refund remains entirely with Apple.

7. Right of Withdrawal (Instruction for Consumers)

In certain jurisdictions, consumers may have a legal right to withdraw from a contract for digital content. For purchases made on the Apple App Store, this right is governed by Apple's terms and conditions.

Loss of Withdrawal Right: By purchasing a subscription and consenting to the immediate delivery of the digital content (i.e., access to Premium Services), you typically acknowledge and agree that you lose your right to withdraw from the contract once the service begins. Apple's purchase flow is designed to obtain this consent.

Any request to exercise a potential right of withdrawal must be directed to Apple via the refund process described in Section 6. Apple will determine your eligibility based on its policies and the applicable laws of your region.

Email: phonetracker.feedback@outlook.com

While we cannot process refunds, we can help troubleshoot issues and provide technical context that may support your refund request to Apple. Contacting us does not constitute a formal refund request.
